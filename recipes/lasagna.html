Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of flat pasta, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, and melted cheese. It's a hearty and comforting baked casserole that's loved for its savory flavors and satisfying textures.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles (oven-ready or cooked
  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage (or a combination)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    • In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
    • Add chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Sauté until the onion is translucent.
    • Add chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Sauté until the onion is translucent.
  2. Prepare the Ricotta Mixture:
    • In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, chopped parsley, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Mix well to create a creamy mixture.
  3. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
    • Place three lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
    • Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
    • Sprinkle about 1/3 of the shredded mozzarella cheese over the ricotta layer.
    • Repeat the layers: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese.
    • Top with a final layer of meat sauce, remaining shredded mozzarella cheese, and grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add Béchamel Sauce:
    • Carefully pour the béchamel sauce over the top layer of cheese. Use a spatula to spread it evenly.
  5. Bake the Lasagna:
    • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil, tenting it slightly to prevent the cheese from sticking.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for about 25 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and bubbling.
  6. Serve:
    • Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for about 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. This allows the layers to set and makes it easier to cut into neat portions
    • Serve the lasagna with a side salad and some crusty bread for a complete meal.

Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
