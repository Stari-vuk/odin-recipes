Burger

Description

A timeless favorite, the classic beef burger is a juicy and flavorful option that satisfies your burger cravings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (80% lean)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise)

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the patties on the grill or pan and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side for medium-rare, or adjust the cooking time according to your preference.
  4. In the last minute of cooking, add cheese slices on top of each patty if desired, and cover to melt.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or pan for a minute until lightly browned.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing lettuce leaves on the bottom bun, followed by the cooked patty, sliced tomatoes, and onions. Add condiments of your choice.
  7. Top with the other half of the bun and serve immediately.
